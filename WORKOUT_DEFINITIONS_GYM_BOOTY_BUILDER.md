## WORKOUT 1: GLUTE SCULPT
**Focus:** Instagram-famous exercises for shape + definition

### BEGINNER (3 exercises - learn the basics)
**Philosophy:** Master fundamental movements women actually do
1. **Glute Bridge** (3 sets x 15 reps) - Foundation hip thrust, learn glute squeeze
2. **Reverse Lunge** (3 sets x 15 reps per leg) - Forward-leaning for glute emphasis
3. **Standing Glute Kickback** (3 sets x 15 reps per leg) - Basic kickback pattern

**Why this works:** This is what every female beginner does: bridges (hip thrust prep), lunges (glute emphasis), kickbacks (isolation). Sommer Ray does these exact exercises. High reps (15) for pump and muscle endurance.

**What influencers say:** "Start with bodyweight, FEEL the glutes working before adding weight!" - Whitney Simmons
