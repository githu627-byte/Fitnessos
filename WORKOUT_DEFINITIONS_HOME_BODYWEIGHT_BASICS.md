## WORKOUT 1: FULL BODY (Complete home workout)

### BEGINNER (3 exercises - fundamental patterns)
**Philosophy:** Learn basic bodyweight movements
1. **Air Squat** (3 sets x 12 reps) - Lower body push pattern
2. **Push-Up** (3 sets x 12 reps) - Upper body push (can do on knees)
3. **Plank** (3 sets x 30 seconds) - Core stability

**Why this works:** Research shows beginners need: 1 lower + 1 upper + 1 core. Air squats teach squat pattern. Push-ups (or knee push-ups) build upper body. Planks develop core foundation. This is the EXACT structure from Nerd Fitness beginner program.

**Home workout tip:** No equipment needed. 20-minute workout. Can do anywhere.

---

### INTERMEDIATE (5 exercises - complete body stimulus)
**Philosophy:** Add complexity and volume
1. **Jump Squat** (4 sets x 10 reps) - Explosive lower body
2. **Push-Up** (4 sets x 12 reps) - Upper push (full ROM)
3. **Inverted Row** (4 sets x 10 reps) - Upper pull (use table/bar)
4. **Static Lunge** (4 sets x 10 reps per leg) - Unilateral lower
5. **Mountain Climber** (4 sets x 30 seconds) - Core + cardio

**Why this works:** Popular home circuits use: explosive movement (jump squat) + push + pull + unilateral + core/cardio finisher. Inverted rows can be done under a table. Mountain climbers add cardio element. This is 2026 circuit training format.

**Home workout tip:** Use sturdy table for rows. 30-min workout.

---

### ADVANCED (7 exercises - high-intensity calisthenics)
**Philosophy:** Maximum bodyweight challenge
1. **Pistol Squat** (4 sets x 8 reps per leg) - Advanced single-leg strength
2. **Decline Push-Up** (4 sets x 12 reps) - Harder push angle (feet elevated)
3. **Pull-Up** (4 sets x 8 reps) - Upper pull mastery
4. **Jumping Lunge** (4 sets x 10 reps per leg) - Explosive unilateral
5. **Burpee** (4 sets x 10 reps) - Full body explosive
6. **Mountain Climber** (4 sets x 40 seconds) - Core + conditioning
7. **Plank** (4 sets x 60 seconds) - Core endurance

**Why this works:** Advanced calisthenics = hardest bodyweight variations. Pistol squats are elite leg exercise. Decline push-ups increase difficulty. Pull-ups require bar but are king of bodyweight pulling. Burpees for conditioning. This is 28-day calisthenics challenge level.

**Home workout tip:** Need pull-up bar. 40-45 min high-intensity session.

## PROGRESSIVE OVERLOAD - HOW TO PROGRESS WITHOUT WEIGHTS

### **Beginner ‚Üí Intermediate:**
1. **Increase reps** (12 ‚Üí 15 ‚Üí 20)
2. **Add sets** (3 sets ‚Üí 4 sets)
3. **Harder variations** (knee push-ups ‚Üí regular push-ups)
4. **Add explosive** (air squat ‚Üí jump squat)

### **Intermediate ‚Üí Advanced:**
1. **Tempo control** (3-second eccentric, 1-second pause)
2. **Advanced variations** (push-up ‚Üí decline ‚Üí archer ‚Üí one-arm progression)
3. **Reduce rest** (90 sec ‚Üí 60 sec ‚Üí 45 sec between sets)
4. **Add plyometrics** (squat ‚Üí jump squat ‚Üí tuck jump)

### **Advanced Techniques:**
1. **Unilateral progression** (two-leg ‚Üí single-leg variations)
2. **Isometric holds** (bottom of squat hold 10 seconds)
3. **Pause reps** (pause 2 seconds at hardest point)
4. **1.5 reps** (full rep + half rep = 1.5)
5. **Density training** (more reps in same time)
