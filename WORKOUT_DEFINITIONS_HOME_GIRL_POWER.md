## WORKOUT 1: GLUTE SCULPT (Home booty + lower body focus)
**Focus:** Lower body emphasis with glute specialization

### BEGINNER (3 exercises - bodyweight lower body)
**Philosophy:** Build lower body foundation
1. **Glute Bridge** (3 sets x 20 reps) - Hip thrust foundation
2. **Sumo Squat** (3 sets x 15 reps) - Wide stance lower body
3. **Reverse Lunge** (3 sets x 12 reps per leg) - Unilateral leg work

**Why this works:** Women at home prioritize lower body. Glute bridges = foundation. Sumo squats = wide stance (glute emphasis). Reverse lunges = unilateral work. This is beginner-friendly lower body focus.

**Home workout tip:** 15-20 min. No equipment. Can do daily.

---

### INTERMEDIATE (5 exercises - full lower body + core)
**Philosophy:** Complete lower body development
1. **Single Leg Glute Bridge** (4 sets x 15 reps per side) - Unilateral hip thrust
2. **Jump Squat** (4 sets x 12 reps) - Explosive lower power
3. **Walking Lunge** (4 sets x 12 reps per leg) - Dynamic unilateral
4. **Curtsy Lunge** (4 sets x 12 reps per leg) - Glute medius (side glute)
5. **Mountain Climber** (4 sets x 30 seconds) - Core + cardio

**Why this works:** Chloe Ting programs use this structure: glute work + explosive (jump squat) + lunges (2 variations) + core finisher. This hits glutes from ALL angles. Mountain climbers add cardio element.

**Home workout tip:** 25-30 min. Add mini band for extra burn.

**Home workout tip:** 35-40 min. Advanced only. Maximum lower volume.

## WORKOUT 2: UPPER/LOWER SPLIT - UPPER BODY (Home upper body for women)
**Focus:** Toned arms, shoulders, back (no chest focus)

### BEGINNER (3 exercises - basic upper body)
**Philosophy:** Learn fundamental upper body patterns
1. **Incline Push-Up** (3 sets x 12 reps) - Easier push angle (hands elevated on chair)
2. **Inverted Row** (3 sets x 10 reps) - Horizontal pull (under table)
3. **Plank** (3 sets x 30 seconds) - Core stability

**Why this works:** Women's upper body at home = modified push-ups (incline easier) + pulling (rows under table) + core (plank). This builds foundation for toned arms without heavy weights.

**Home workout tip:** Use chair for incline push-ups, sturdy table for rows. 15-20 min.

**Why this works:** Rowan Row's 3.4M view video uses this structure: 2 push variations + rows + dynamic plank + cardio finisher. This hits arms (push-ups = triceps, rows = biceps), shoulders (plank taps), core (mountain climbers).

**Home workout tip:** 25-30 min. No equipment or just table.
