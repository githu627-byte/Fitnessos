# GYM CIRCUITS - PERFECTED WORKOUT DEFINITIONS

## RESEARCH SUMMARY
Based on 2026 circuit training research from:
- Muscle & Fitness HIIT protocols
- ACE Fitness circuit training guidelines
- PureGym functional HIIT workouts
- Men's Fitness circuit programming

**Key Principles:**
1. **Work:Rest Ratios** - Beginner: 30s:30s | Intermediate: 40s:20s | Advanced: 45s:15s
2. **Minimal rest between exercises** - Circuit format keeps heart rate elevated
3. **Multiple rounds** - Complete all exercises, rest 60-90s, repeat
4. **Compound movements** - Maximize calorie burn + muscle engagement
5. **EPOC effect** - Afterburn continues burning calories for hours

**Why Circuits Work:**
- Research shows 20-minute HIIT can burn 150-400 calories
- Active recovery: one muscle works while another rests
- Time-efficient: Strength + cardio in one session
- Proven to reduce fat mass + increase VO2 max

## 1. FULL BODY BLAST (3 rounds)

### BEGINNER (4 exercises - 30s work / 30s rest)
**Philosophy:** Learn movement patterns with time-based training
1. **Marching In Place** (30s work / 30s rest) - Cardio warm-up, get heart rate up
2. **Push-Ups** (30s work / 30s rest) - Upper body push, chest + triceps
3. **Air Squat** (30s work / 30s rest) - Lower body squat pattern
4. **Plank** (30s work / 30s rest) - Core stability, anti-extension

**Why this works:** All circuit research shows beginners need 1:1 work:rest ratio. This covers all movement patterns: cardio (march), push (push-ups), squat (air squat), core (plank). Simple and effective full-body stimulus.

**Total time:** ~8 minutes (2 rounds)

---

### INTERMEDIATE (6 exercises - 40s work / 20s rest)
**Philosophy:** Increase intensity, mix compound movements
1. **High Knee** (40s work / 20s rest) - Cardio burst, elevate heart rate
2. **Dumbbell Bench Press** (40s work / 20s rest) - Upper push, chest compound
3. **Goblet Squat** (40s work / 20s rest) - Lower squat, add load
4. **Dumbbell Row** (40s work / 20s rest) - Upper pull, back work
5. **Walking Lunge** (40s work / 20s rest) - Lower unilateral, leg burn
6. **Mountain Climber** (40s work / 20s rest) - Core + cardio finisher

**Why this works:** Research shows 40s:20s ratio maximizes fat burn. This circuit alternates: cardio â†’ upper push â†’ lower push â†’ upper pull â†’ lower unilateral â†’ core/cardio. All major muscle groups + cardiovascular stimulus = complete workout.

**Total time:** ~18 minutes (3 rounds)

### ADVANCED (8 exercises - 45s work / 15s rest)
**Philosophy:** Maximum intensity, explosive movements
1. **High Knee Sprint** (45s work / 15s rest) - Max cardio intensity
2. **Barbell Bench Press** (45s work / 15s rest) - Heavy upper push
3. **Jump Squat** (45s work / 15s rest) - Explosive lower power

**Why this works:** 45s:15s is ELITE HIIT ratio - proven maximum calorie burn. This uses heavy compounds (BB bench, BB row, pull-ups) mixed with plyometrics (jump squats, burpees). Research shows this creates massive EPOC (afterburn effect). Instagram fitness models use this exact structure.
